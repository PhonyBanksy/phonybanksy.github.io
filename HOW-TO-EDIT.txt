==============================================================
 HOW TO CHANGE THE WORDS & DETAILS ON THE PRIME COLLECTIONS SITE
==============================================================

You only ever need to open ONE file:

        content.js

Open it with Notepad (right-click > Open with > Notepad) or any text editor.

--------------------------------------------------------------
THE GOLDEN RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------
1. Change ONLY the words between "quotation marks".

      email:  "info@primecollections.co.uk",
                ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^ change this bit only

2. Do NOT change the word before the colon (email, phone, heading...).
   That is a label the website needs.

3. Keep every " quote, every , comma and every { } bracket where it is.

4. Words in {curly brackets} like {phone}, {email} or {company} fill
   themselves in automatically. Set your phone/email/company ONCE at the
   top of the file and they update everywhere they appear.

--------------------------------------------------------------
YOUR MAIN DETAILS (top of content.js)
--------------------------------------------------------------
   company     - your business name
   phone       - your phone number
   email       - your email address
   established - the year shown as "Est. ..."
   regNumber   - Companies House registration number
   regAddress  - registered office address
   year        - the year in the copyright line

Change these and they update across the whole website by themselves.

--------------------------------------------------------------
AFTER YOU EDIT
--------------------------------------------------------------
Save the file. The website updates once the file is uploaded/published
(Marius or your web person does that, or it happens automatically).

--------------------------------------------------------------
IF SOMETHING LOOKS WRONG
--------------------------------------------------------------
It is almost always a missing " quotation mark or , comma.
Undo your last change (Ctrl + Z) and save again.
Good news: if the file has a mistake, the website simply keeps showing the
last correct wording — it will not go blank.

--------------------------------------------------------------
NOTE
--------------------------------------------------------------
The "i18n" folder holds old translation files and is NOT used right now.
Ignore it. Everything you need is in content.js.
